LASAGNA

Delicious Lasagna Dish

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

INDREDIENTS

  • Lsagna noodles
  • 4. Cloves garlic
  • Ground beef
  • Extra-virgin olive oil

COOKING STEPS

  1. Our sauce is made with a mix of ground beef and pork. This is OPTIONAL. If you do not like pork you can definitely use beef to replace it, but the pork does add a traditional lasagna flavour to the sauce.
  2. Most sauces use crushed tomatoes as a base, however we love mixing in a bottle of Passata (or American tomato sauce) to add richness.
  3. Using tomato paste results in a thick and rich sauce. Do not skip this ingredient! We love using one with garlic and herbs, but plain is also fine.
